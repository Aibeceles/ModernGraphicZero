Our client, a payroll service company, is looking for an experienced Data Analyst who will scrutinize the company’s data and reports to ensure the quality and accuracy of data used for insights in business decisions.

The ideal candidate must have a broad knowledge of finance and reporting and must be an expert data analyst to provide coherent information to streamline business performance and identify quality control plans for business improvements.

Responsibilities:

    Interpreting data, analyzing results using statistical techniques to turn data into information, information into insight, and insight into business decisions
    Developing and implementing data analyses, data collection systems, and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
    Acquiring data from primary or secondary data sources and maintaining databases using tools and techniques to visualize data in easy-to-understand formats, such as diagrams and graphs
    Develop and implement databases, data collection systems, data analytics, and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
    Communicate with stakeholders to understand data content and business requirements
    Work with management to prioritize business and information needs and define new process improvement opportunities

    5+ years proven working experience as a Data Analyst or Business Data Analyst
    Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining, segmentation techniques, statistical methodologies, and data analysis techniques
    Strong knowledge of and experience with reporting packages (Business Objects etc), databases (SQL, etc), In-depth knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets (Excel, SPSS, SAS, etc)
    Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
    Strong verbal and written communication skills to effectively share findings with shareholders, ability adept to queries, report writing, and presenting findings
